What is attar perfume?

Attar is an oil-based, alcohol-free perfume made from natural and aromatic ingredients. It is known for its long-lasting fragrance and skin-friendly nature.

Is attar better than perfume?

Attar is preferred by many due to its alcohol-free composition, longer staying power, and suitability for daily use compared to alcohol-based perfumes.

How long does attar last?

A good quality attar can last between 6 to 12 hours on the skin, depending on fragrance type and body chemistry.

Is attar alcohol free?

Yes, traditional attars are completely alcohol-free and are made using natural fragrance extraction methods.
